SAVE FORMAT FOR A MONTHLY BUDGET:
=================================
(budgetHandler)
[types list...]
[
budgetHandler rows...
]

FIRST SECTION: (budgetHandler) (double)

SECOND SECTION: [types list...] (String[])
List of "types" separated by a tab each.

THIRD SECTION: [budgetHandler rows...] (BudgetRow[])
List of budgetHandler rows. Each field of a budgetHandler row

NOTES & IMPLEMENTATION:
=======================
* A budgetHandler row is in the format of:
[expense category] [date] [budgetHandler category] [name] [money]

Where:
[expense category]: Either "Fixed" or "Variable" to identify expense category.
[date]: Date in DAY-MONTH format.
[budgetHandler category]: A budgetHandler category, should be one of [types list...] tokens, but the
reader will NOT enforce it.
[name]: Name of the budgetHandler row.
[money]: Numerical value.
